About MoodSky

MoodSky 是一款专注于个人情绪记录与回顾的 App。你可以用简单的方式记录每日心情，在日历与时间线中回顾自己的情绪变化，所有内容都只保存在你的设备与你的 iCloud 账户中。

我们相信隐私是基本权利。MoodSky 不收集、不上传、不分析任何可识别你身份的数据，也不包含第三方分析或广告 SDK。

✓ 数据仅本地存储 · ✓ iCloud 同步（可选）· ✓ 零用户信息收集

MoodSky is an app for logging and reviewing your mood. Record how you feel each day, look back on your mood over time in the calendar and timeline—all stored only on your device and in your iCloud account.

隐私政策 (Privacy Policy)

Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026年2月

Last updated: February 2026

1. 概述 (Overview)

MoodSky（「本应用」）尊重您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何对待您的数据。核心原则：您的数据存储在您的设备上，我们不会收集任何可识别您身份的信息。

2. 数据存储 (Data Storage)

  • 本地存储：您创建的心情记录、标签、设置等数据均保存在您使用的那台设备本地。我们不会将这些数据发送到自己的服务器。
  • iCloud 同步：若您选择开启 iCloud 同步，数据会通过 Apple 的 iCloud 服务在您自己的设备之间同步。该过程由 Apple 处理，我们无法访问您 iCloud 账户中的内容。

3. 我们不收集的信息 (What We Do Not Collect)

我们不收集您的姓名、邮箱、电话号码、设备标识符、位置信息或任何其他可用于识别您身份的数据。我们不在应用内嵌入第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

4. 数据安全 (Data Security)

因为数据主要存储在您的设备上，设备本身的安全（如锁屏密码、生物识别）即构成数据安全的重要部分。若您使用 iCloud 同步，数据会受 Apple iCloud 安全策略与您 Apple ID 安全设置的保护。

5. 儿童隐私 (Children’s Privacy)

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童主动收集个人信息。若您为家长或监护人并对儿童使用本应用有疑问，欢迎通过下方联系方式与我们联系。

6. 政策变更 (Changes to This Policy)

我们可能会偶尔更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本会发布在本页面，并注明「最后更新」日期。继续使用本应用即表示您接受更新后的政策。

1. Overview

MoodSky (“the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we treat your data. Our core principle: Your data stays on your device, and we do not collect any information that identifies you.

2. Data Storage

  • Local storage: Mood entries, tags, and settings you create are stored locally on the device you use. We do not send this data to our own servers.
  • iCloud sync: If you enable iCloud sync, data is synced between your own devices via Apple’s iCloud. This is handled by Apple; we do not have access to the contents of your iCloud account.

3. What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, device identifiers, location, or any other data that can identify you. We do not embed third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs in the app.

4. Data Security

Because your data is stored primarily on your device, your device’s own security (e.g. passcode, biometrics) is a key part of keeping it safe. If you use iCloud sync, your data is also protected by Apple’s iCloud security and your Apple ID settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about your child’s use of the app, please contact us using the details below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
